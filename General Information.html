General Info

  • The festival is all ages. Eight (8) years and younger are free in GA. Two (2) years and younger are free in VIP.
  • Attendees 17 years or younger must be accompanied by an adult age 18 years or older. Accompanying adult can be a friend, family member or guardian.
  • Everyone is subject to search at Festival entrance gates and in the campgrounds.
  • Wristband is void if tampered with or damaged.
  • NO wristband sharing.
  • Day Parking is available at Dover International Speedway 2 hours prior to gates opening and 1 hour after the show ends each day.
  • Lineup and schedule is subject to change.
  • Festival is rain or shine.

Breakin the Rules

  • You will not receive another wristband if it was intentionally tampered with.
  • You will be escorted from the premises if your behavior is deeemed innaproriate (i.e. sexual harrassment, acts of hate, destruction of Firefly property).
  • If you do not comply with searches, you will not be let on the premises of the festival.
  • The festival reserves the right to involve the proper authorities if we believe the safety of our guests is compromised.
