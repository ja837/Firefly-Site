Experience

Experience

The Treehouse

Tucked away in the trees, this intimate stage is the perfect place to escape the sun and catch up-and-coming bands, acoustic performances and exclusive interviews.

The Nest

The music doesn’t stop until we say so. Experience back to back sets all weekend long from DJ’s nested high within the Woodland trees

Central Market

Shop an array of unique boutiques featuring clothing, jewelry and hand crafted memorabilia.

Eat and Drink

Nothing goes better with great music than refreshing drinks and delicious eats. Firefly is full of tasty treats from high-end cocktails and late night pizza to vegan and gluten free entrees and gourmet delicacies.

Good Hub

We invite you to the Good Hub, where you can connect with different non-profit organizations that are working to raise awareness and educate attendees about their individual causes. Check back at a later date to learn about the participating organizations!
